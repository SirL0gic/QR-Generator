What is a QR code?

A QR Code is a two-dimensional barcode that typically consists of patterns of black and white pixels. They were created by Denso Wave, a Japanese division of Toyota supplier Denso, to mark components and speed up the logistics of their auto manufacturing. Due to the widespread use of smartphones, it is now being used in mobile marketing. Quick Response, or "QR," is a term that refers to the instant access to the data that is concealed in the Code.

What are the benefits of using QR codes?

They are becoming more and more well-liked due to their adaptability. By using them, you can increase customer engagement with images or videos, collect feedback for the improvement of your goods or services, or even advertise your company through events and discounts. With just one scan, all of these tasks can be completed!

How to use QR codes?

You might already have a built-in QR Code reader or scanner, depending on your device. Your phone's camera app should be open.
